TOMORROW X TOGETHER (read as Tomorrow by Together; commonly known as TXT) is a five-member boy group under Big Hit Music .The members don't have any official fixed positions, except for the leader. They debuted on March 4, 2019, with their first mini album The Dream Chapter: STAR at Mnet's 'Debut Celebration Show'. According to their website, the meaning behind "Tomorrow by Together" is five individuals who "come together under one dream in hopes of building a better tomorrow".
