Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. Formed in 2009, Imagine Dragons first revealed their limitless passion and genre-bending sensibilities with a series of independently released EPs that quickly earned a grassroots following.

Over the last half-decade, Imagine Dragons have emerged as one of the biggest and most compelling rock bands in the world.
