Stray Kids

"Step Out! Hello, We are Stray Kids" â€” Stray Kids' Introduction


Stray Kids is an eight-member boy group under JYP Entertainment. Formed through the competition reality show of the same name and debuted on March 25, 2018, with the mini album I Am Not. The name of the group is named by the team members. The group name expresses their determination to break the existing mold and go one step forward to be out of the ordinary.
